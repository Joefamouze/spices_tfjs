How to use the website to train your computer

Click on the buttons below the image frame to add a picture to your dataset.
Make sure to click on each button atleast 30 times to get a good result, you could feed the model any image
When you are done feeding the model with images, click on train network
To check the progress if you are inquisitive, go to your developer console and check the console for the logs
When you are done training, click on predict, then show the webcam any of the images you trained it with,
it should predict correctly the image you show it.
